Smart Shop Refund and Return Policy

1. Return Window: Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery.
2. Condition: Items must be unused, in original packaging with tags attached.
3. Refund Processing: Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after the returned item is received.
4. Non-returnable Items: Perishable goods, personal care items, and downloadable software cannot be returned.
5. Shipping Costs: Return shipping is free for defective items. For other returns, the customer bears the shipping cost.
6. Exchange: Items can be exchanged for a different size or color within 30 days, subject to availability.
