Smart Shop Shipping and Delivery Policy

1. Standard Shipping: 3-7 business days, free for orders over $50.
2. Express Shipping: 1-2 business days, $9.99 flat rate.
3. Same-Day Delivery: Available in select cities, $14.99. Orders must be placed before 12:00 PM.
4. Tracking: All orders include tracking. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order is shipped.
5. International Shipping: Available to select countries. Delivery times vary (7-21 business days). Additional customs fees may apply.
6. Delivery Issues: If your package is lost or damaged during transit, please contact us within 48 hours.
